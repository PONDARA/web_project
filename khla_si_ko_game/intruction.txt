+Processing of game:
-Table:
    	Four columns
    	Four rows
-Need two people to play:
 	one person have twelve cows
    	One person have four tigers
-Takes turn :
    	 Puts four tigers first (person1)
    	 Then can put a cow 
    	 anywhere(person2) 
   	 Moves the tiger to left or right 
  	 or below or above side(person2)
	Continue to take turn
-Condition:
	A tiger can jump just only one a cow  
	If a tiger can jump over a cow 
    	so a cow is deleted (tiger eat cow)
	If cows can besiege the tiger 
    	so a tiger is deleted
-Win and lose:
	Loser is the person that has tigers or cows were deleted all
